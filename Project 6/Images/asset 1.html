Overview Royal Green County Villa Sector 62, Gurugram

Royal Green County Villas in Sector 40, Bahadurgarh, Haryana, is an exquisite residential project by the renowned Royal Group. Offering a seamless blend of opulence, comfort, and convenience, this development boasts meticulously designed villas ranging from 3 to 6 bedrooms. Each villa is adorned with modern amenities like modular kitchens, imported marble flooring, and deluxe bathrooms.

Residents are pampered with a host of facilities, including a sprawling central park with a children's play area, a clubhouse featuring a pool, gym, and indoor games room, plus a basketball and tennis court. The round-the-clock security system with CCTV surveillance ensures peace of mind.

Strategically positioned in the heart of Bahadurgarh, Royal Green County Villas enjoys proximity to key amenities and infrastructural hubs. It also enjoys excellent connectivity to Delhi and other major NCR cities.

Experience the epitome of luxury living with Royal Green County Villas – where every moment is steeped in elegance and comfort.
